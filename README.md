### Hi, I'm Enrique Catalá 👋

I'm a Computer Engineer and Executive Professional with more than 20 years of experience in the Data Platform space. Currently, I serve as **Principal Data Architect** for **Lidl Plus** (one of the biggest Loyalty programs in Europe) at _SCRM International Lidl Hub / Schwarz Global Services Barcelona_. My dedication to technical excellence and the community led to a decade-long tenure as a **Microsoft Data Platform MVP**.

In my role, I technically lead a team of +100 data and platform engineers in shaping the data architecture. My work harmonizes strategic guidance with technical stewardship, driving our data capabilities to high levels of scalability, agility, and innovation. 

I specialize in designing and building high-performance **Data Platforms**, large-scale **Streaming** solutions, and establishing decentralized architectures guided by **Data Contracts** and **Data Mesh** principles. Additionally, I am actively involved in designing and deploying **agentic architectures** on top of our data platforms to enable **conversational analytics** via semantic layers using **LangGraph** agents.

#### 🔑 Key Responsibilities & Focus:
- **Technical Strategic Leadership**: Spearheading the design and adoption of a cutting-edge **Data Mesh** architecture. Collaborating across the Schwarz Group to establish a federated, scalable, and self-service data platform.
- **Data Platforms, Streaming & Governance**: Building scalable real-time and batch ingestion systems with **Spark, Databricks, and Event Hubs**, enforced by robust **Data Contracts**.
- **Agentic Architectures & AI**: Leading the strategy and implementation of agentic AI systems using **LangGraph** on top of our data platforms, enabling **conversational analytics** through semantic layers and complex multi-agent orchestration.
- **Standards & Best Practices**: Establishing robust frameworks for data ingestion, modeling, and consumption to uphold the highest quality and engineering standards.
- **Tech-Business Integration**: Merging technical and business insights in high-level strategic dialogues with business leaders to align technology with corporate goals.
- **Innovation & Research**: Continually researching emerging technologies and architectural methodologies to keep our data platforms at the technological forefront.
